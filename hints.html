How to not die quite as fast

  1. You can climb the walls and jump off at any height
  2. Squish foxes by tricking them into jumping into a falling box
  3. You can increase fall speed with the down arrow
  4. Fall on a Fox that is facing downwards to put it to sleep (risky)
  5. Sleeping foxes don't wake up unless pushed. So just don't push them.
  6. Try and kill each fox before the next one appears, when they accumulate it gets hard very fast
  7. Sideways collisions with boxes are not fatal
  8. Any collision that is not fatal allows you to jump again
  9. You can jump in mid air once after hitting an object
