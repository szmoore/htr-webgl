What The Hell Is This?

Humphrey The Rabbit is a game where you avoid Boxes and Squish Foxes which must also be avoided.

Try and stay alive until the music is finished and the level is done.

It is not supposed to be easy but when played properly you do stand a chance.

HtR is in a state of development and may change without notice at any time.
